## Why the puff sleeve, and why now?

There is a reason the fashion world keeps reaching for volume at the shoulder. A puff sleeve does something no other silhouette can: it reframes the collarbone, narrows the waist by sheer optical magic, and makes the wearer look like she just stepped out of a watercolor. It is the most quietly flattering trend in the boho lexicon — generous to every body, gentle to every mood, and endlessly easy to style.

This season, the puff is softer than the dramatic balloon-sleeves we saw in 2021. Think: gentle gathers. Airy cotton. A shoulder that breathes rather than announces. The new puff is less about statement and more about suggestion — a little whisper of volume that says *I am dressed for joy today*.
